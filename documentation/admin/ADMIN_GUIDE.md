# Mwanda Mzedu SACCO: Administrator’s Migration Guide 🚀

Welcome to Mwanda Mzedu SACCO! This guide outlines the 5-phase migration process to our platform, with specific focus on our unique lending policies.

---

## 🏗 Phase 1: Foundation (System Rules)
Setup your GL, Payment Accounts, Fees, and Products. 

### ⚠️ Special Loan Rules
When setting up **Loan Products**, remember our specific policies:
1.  **Membership Maturity:** Loans are only available to members who have been with the SACCO for at least **6 months**.
2.  **First Loan Limit:** For a member's first loan, the application is restricted to **80% of their total savings**.

---

## 👥 Phase 2: Member Migration
Import your members via Single Invite, Bulk Form, or CSV mass upload.

---

## 💰 Phase 3: Savings & Fees Migration
Migrate existing balances. This is crucial for Mwanda Mzedu as loan eligibility is tied to savings history.

---

## 🏦 Phase 4: Loan Migration & Lifecycle
Migrate active loans and manage the ongoing application flow.

---

## 📊 Phase 5: Reports & Validation
Verify your migration using the reporting engine.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Policy Enforcement:** Sprout automatically checks for the 6-month maturity and 80% first-loan rule. Ensure member 'Joined Date' is accurate during migration.
