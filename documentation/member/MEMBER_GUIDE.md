# Mwanda Mzedu SACCO: Member’s User Guide

Welcome! Manage your savings and credit with the Mwanda Mzedu Member Portal.

---

## 📈 Loans & Credit

### Our Unique Lending Policy
To ensure sustainability, Mwanda Mzedu follows these rules:
1.  **6-Month Rule:** You must be a member for at least **6 months** before applying for your first loan.
2.  **First Loan Limit:** Your first loan application is limited to **80% of your current savings**. Subsequent loans follow the standard SACCO multipliers.

### The Loan Application Journey
1.  **Check Eligibility:** Ensure you've met the 6-month requirement.
2.  **Select Product & Apply:** Use the calculator to see your limits.
3.  **Amendment & Guarantors:** Respond to Admin changes and invite guarantors if needed.
4.  **Final Submission:** Submit once fully guaranteed.

### Repayments & Tracking
*   **Balance Updates:** Payments are reconciled and portal balances are typically updated by the **end of the day**.
*   **Schedule:** View your next payment date in the Loans dashboard.
